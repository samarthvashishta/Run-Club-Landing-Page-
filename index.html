Got Questions?

How much does it cost? expand_more
Our weekly community runs are 100% free. We believe fitness should be accessible to everyone. We occasionally host ticketed workshops or charity events, but the primary runs are always open to all.
What if I'm too slow? expand_more
No such thing! We have dedicated "Tail Walkers" and pacer groups for every speed. We start together and we finish together.
Do I need special gear? expand_more
Just a pair of comfortable running shoes and clothes you can move in! We recommend moisture-wicking fabric for longer runs, but your favorite t-shirt works just fine for a start.
